Frisian Sailing Company App


Assignment Info

This was my fourth project of my first year in college. This time we had a real product owner, that gave us requirements into how the app should function. She wanted an mobile app where customers of her company could track activities or any points of interest nearby. The intent of this app was: that users could use this app whenever their ship arrived at it's destination.


My tasks

In this project i was responsible for the front-end of the application and making the app as user-friendly as possible. I was also responsible for the creation of the database and all commands to gather information from the database.


What have i learnt?

In this project i learnt advanced techniques in Xamarin.Forms. I also delve further into designing an user-friendly Interface, by implementing the 10 heuristics of Nielsen. Besides coding i also learnt how to create an UI using Figma. I also learnt how to create a good-looking database, and how to gather data from the database. And finally i learnt how to use the GPS on the phone to show the phone's current location, and perform an action with that location.
